Title: Transaction Log Scenarios

Scenario 1: Transaction log created after Joey deposits $100 into account
  1) Joey receives a check from his job for $100.
  2) He goes to the BlueBank to deposit the check into his account.
  3) Joey deposits the check for $100 into his account.
  4) A log entry is entered into his account stating "Joey - deposit 100.00"
  5) Joey leaves the BlueBank.

Scenario 2: Transaction log created after Joey withdraws $25 from his account
  1) Joey needs money to put gas into his car.
  2) He goes to the Blue Bank to withdraw $25 from his account for gas and spending money.
  3) Joey requests to withdraw $25 from the bank.
  4) He receives the money and a transaction log is generated stating "Joey - withdraw 25.00"
  5) Joey leaves the bank.

Scenario 3: Transaction log created after Joey transfers money to Diane
  1) Diane asked Joey to borrow $15.
  2) Joey says yes and logs into his Blue Bank account.
  3) He chooses the option to Transfer money and enters Diane's account information.
  4) Joey enters $15 to transfer to Diane.
  5) He okays the transaction and $15 is transferred to Diane's account.
  6) A transaction log is generated stating "Joey - transfer 15.00 Diane"
  7) Joey logs out of his account.
